# HedgeFun

A 2D side-scrolling simulation game about growing and managing a hedgerow ecosystem. Plant native UK hedge species, watch them grow through the seasons, and attract wildlife — from underground moles to treetop birds.

## About

HedgeFun is a relaxing arcade-style sim with no win condition. Your score increases as you cultivate a thriving hedgerow: attracting new creatures, helping them build families, and nurturing a diverse ecosystem.

- **Plant & prune** native UK hedge species
- **Seasonal gameplay** driven by solstices, equinoxes, and moon phases
- **Anthropomorphic wildlife** inhabits different vertical layers of your hedge
- **ASCII art** visuals with colored glyphs and animations
